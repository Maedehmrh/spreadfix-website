Common Questions

A few things people usually ask before submitting.

How do I send you my file? ▾
Just use the form below — you can attach your Excel or CSV file directly, or describe your issue if you don't have a file yet. I'll follow up by email.
How long does it take? ▾
Most projects are completed within 24–48 hours. If you have a tight deadline, mention it in the form and I'll do my best to accommodate it.
How does payment work? ▾
After reviewing your project, I'll email you a custom quote. If you approve it, I'll send a secure payment link so you can pay safely by card online. Work begins once payment is confirmed.

I offer one round of revisions on every project. If something isn't right, just let me know and I'll fix it.

Absolutely. Your file is only used to complete your project and is never shared with anyone else.
